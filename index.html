Ready Answers for Tough Questions
"We already use spreadsheets and it's working fine."
Response: Spreadsheets work — until a term-end rush, a lost file, or a fee dispute hits. AIMS keeps everything in one place, with a history that can't be accidentally deleted. And you can keep using what you know while we transition gradually.
"We can't afford this right now."
Response: That's exactly why we offer the free trial first. Use it for one term at zero cost, and let the fee recovery alone show you whether it pays for itself. Many schools recover more than the subscription cost in the first month.
"Our internet is unreliable — we can't use online systems."
Response: AIMS was built for that. It runs fully offline and syncs when connection is available. Power or internet issues won't interrupt your operations at all.
"Our staff aren't tech-savvy."
Response: We include hands-on training as part of every setup. Most bursars and teachers are comfortable within one day. The interface was designed to be as simple as a mobile phone.
